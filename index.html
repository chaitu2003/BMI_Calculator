Body Mass Index (BMI)

BMI is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It helps categorize individuals as underweight, normal, overweight, or obese. Use the calculator to find out where you stand and maintain a healthier lifestyle.

  • ✅ Ideal BMI: 18.5 - 24.9
  • ⚠️ Underweight: Less than 18.5
  • ⚠️ Overweight: 25 - 29.9
  • ❌ Obese: 30 and above
